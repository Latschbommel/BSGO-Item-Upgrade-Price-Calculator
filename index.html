BSGO ITEM UPGRADE COST CALCUATOR

by Latschbommel

On this simple page you can easily calculate the amount of cubits needed for your upgrades. You can select the quantity of an item, the level from which you want to upgrade to and the level you want to reach.

Items that have a unique price structure but are not used often, or are not working properly are excluded.

Discounted TK prices may be subject to rounding errors, please notify me if you find incorrect prices.
